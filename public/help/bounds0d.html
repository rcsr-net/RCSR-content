bounds

This section takes numerical input, which can be a single number or a range. Ranges are specified as two numbers separated by a dash, or as one of the symbols < (less than), <= (less or equal), > (greater than) and >= (greater or equal) followed by a number.

Examples:

InputCondition
2 the value (e.g. kinds of vertex) is exactly 2
1-2 the value is between 1 and 2 (inclusive)
< 5 the value is strictly smaller than 5
>= 8 the value is at least 8

coordination

This refers to the coordination of the vertices of the net. To find nets with vertices of a given coordination number, enter that number. To find nets with vertices that have multiple coordination numbers, enter those numbers, separated by commas. * acts as a wild-card (only one allowed).

Examples:

InputCondition
4 all vertices are 4-coordinated
3,4,5 3-, 4-, and 5-coordinated vertices all occur, but no others
3,12,* 3-, 12-, and at least one other coordination

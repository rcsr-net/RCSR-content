td10

td10 is the cumulative sum of the first 10 shells of topological neighbors (i.e. the first 10 terms of the coordination sequence). For structures with more than one kind of vertex the value given is a weighted average over the vertices. Related nets usually have similar values of td10, so this is a good way to find related nets of the same coordination number. Note that, by convention, the coordination sequence starts with 1 for the reference vertex (shell 0) and this is included in the cumulative total given for each vertex.

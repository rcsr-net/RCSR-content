keywords

These are mainly self-explanatory, but note

uniform
Tilings by regular polygons. Sometimes one sees k-uniform for k-nodal tilings by regular polygons.
circle packing
These are tilings that have embeddings with equal edges that correspond to the shortest distance between vertices.
weaving
These patterns appear if, and only if, the keyword and/or symbol are checked. For weaving of threads (coordination number given as 2) the “vertices” are crossing points. For interwoven 2-periodic nets (e.g. sql) the coordinates of the vertices are given.

The main information is the symmetry which is that of one of the 80 layer groups.
